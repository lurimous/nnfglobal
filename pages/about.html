About NNF Global

A Malaysian venture builder with a public-good mandate

NNF Global is a Malaysian project consultancy and venture builder based in Semenyih, Selangor. We originate, structure, finance, and promote ventures that combine commercial strength with tangible public benefit.

  • Parent and exclusive promoter of Hentian Bas Mesra Rakyat and Saté Ria
  • Project structuring including Private Finance Initiative models
  • Experience engaging government ministries and local councils nationwide
